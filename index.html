Hierarchy

The Pope

The Pope is the most powerful amongst the Church and has to keep the balance of the Holy Birds.

The Guides

The Guides are the second most important Members in the Church, they are responsible for helping the people of the faith by aiding them through times of need. They're able to command anyone below them in the Church Hierachy.

The Priests

The Priests have devoted themselves to one of the Holy Birds and are responsible for their local community to praise the Holy Birds.

The Follower

The Follower is the lowest Position and consists of civilians that have devoted themselves to one of the Holy Birds.
